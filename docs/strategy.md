# Alliance Cobrinha LA — Instagram Strategy

This document encodes the strategy from the original *Alliance Cobrinha — Weekly Instagram Program & Production Workflow* deck. It is the source of truth for the skills in this repo.

---

## Weekly content balance

**4 posts per week**, evenly split:

- **2 Adult posts** — high-level technical flow, World Class standards, the "Cobrinha lifestyle." Aesthetic mirrored after Alliance Clark/Manila: clean, minimal, restrained.
- **2 Kids posts** — Future Champions, Summer Programs, character-building. High-energy visuals to capture the family market.

---

## US prime time optimization

| Window | PST | Manila Time |
|--------|-----|-------------|
| Evening Scroll Peak (US PST) | 6–9 PM | 9 AM–12 PM next day |

**Strategy:** post during the Manila morning shift to land live in California prime time.

---

## Adult pillar — "The Standard"

Theme: **Technical Excellence**.

Visuals provided by Prof Cobrinha and Dani. Focus on the intricate details that separate the best from the rest.

Sub-themes:
- High-Contrast Gi Portraits
- Flow Sequence Reels
- Competition Mindset

---

## Kids pillar — "Future Champions"

Theme: capturing the joy and discipline of the youth program.

Assets from Dani focusing on smiling faces and small wins on the mat.

**Goal:** build trust with parents by showing the safe and professional environment of the academy.

### Summer program sub-strategies

- **Beat the Heat** — climate-controlled indoor environment as the best activity for kids during hot summer months
- **Active Defense** — anti-bullying and self-confidence themes while school is out; "The Best Summer Skill"
- **Summer Passes** — special seasonal enrollment offers; high-energy group fun
